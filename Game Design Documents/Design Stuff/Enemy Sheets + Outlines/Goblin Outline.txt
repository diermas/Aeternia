Goblin encounter will begin with at least 1 Goblin, 1 Hob Goblin and 1 Goblin Mage.
The main goal is to blend in a mixture of attack variants from single target to aoe damage.

Goblins are focused expanding their units and sabotage with the chance to destroy a card in a players hand and
bring in reinforcements.

Hob Goblin is to act as the main damage dealer delivering heavy aoe damage, single target damage 
and also being the main front line being able to taunt units on the field to focus him.

Goblin Mage is more of a support role providing heals and cc while also being able to dish 
out a fair bit of damage.

Resistances for all units:
Physical - Normal
Magical - Normal
Fire - Normal
Ice - Normal
Holy - Normal
